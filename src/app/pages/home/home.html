About the Author

Agung is an award-winning writer whose work explores identity, belonging, and the intersections of memory and place. With a background in journalism and literature, their stories weave intimate character portraits with vivid, cinematic worlds.

When not writing, Agung mentors emerging writers and speaks at festivals and universities about craft and the creative process.
