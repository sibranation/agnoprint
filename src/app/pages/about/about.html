About Agung

Award-winning author of contemporary fiction and narrative non-fiction

My Story

Agung is an award-winning writer whose work explores identity, belonging, and the intersections of memory and place. With a background in journalism and literature, their stories weave intimate character portraits with vivid, cinematic worlds.

Born and raised in a multicultural environment, Agung's experiences have deeply influenced their writing, bringing authenticity and depth to themes of cultural identity and personal transformation. Their debut novel, "Echoes of Dawn," received critical acclaim and won the prestigious Literary Prize for Emerging Voices.

When not writing, Agung mentors emerging writers and speaks at festivals and universities about craft and the creative process. They believe in the power of stories to bridge divides and foster understanding in our increasingly connected world.
